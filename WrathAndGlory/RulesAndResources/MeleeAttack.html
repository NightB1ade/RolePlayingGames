--- title: Melee Attack ---
  • Melee attacks use Weapon Skill
  • If no range is listed, effective engagement range is 1m
  • When calculating damage with a melee weapon, add Strength to the weapon’s damage before applying AP to the target’s resilience

Unarmed Strike

  • Damage is Strength +1 ED

Engaged Targets

  • Combatants within their opponent’s melee range are considered engaged in close combat
  • If engaged in close combat, can only attack targets you are engaged with

Reflexive Attacks

  • If an attempt is made to move out of engagement, the opponent may use Reflexive Action to make a Melee Attack
  • Cannot make a Reflexive Attack if the Fall Back Combat Action has been used
