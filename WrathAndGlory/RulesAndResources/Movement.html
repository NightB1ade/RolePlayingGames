--- title: Movement ---

Standard Movement

  • Move Speed in metres
  • Can interrupt movement to take other actions, unless stated otherwise

Running

  • Instead of a Simple Action and cannot run if a Simple Action has already been performed
  • Move double Speed

Sprinting

  • Triple Speed in a straight line
  • Full Round Action

Crawling

  • ½ Speed
  • Prone

Jumping

  • As part of a standard movement can jump 1m high or 1m in distance
  • Making a higher/longer jump is a Simple Action
  • Atheltics (S) Test – DN determined by the GM
  • Long Jump distance = STR metres
  • High Jump distance = ¼ STR metres
  • If you jump into difficult terrain, you must make an additional DN 3 Athletics (S) Test to avoid falling Prone

Swimming

  • ½ Speed
  • If also difficult terrain, GM may ask for an Athletics (S) Test
  • If you become submerged for an extended period of time, fall unconscious, or are Dying whilst swimming, you may begin to drown (see Suffocation p.201)

Flying

  • Flying allows you to move a number of metres equal to either your Speed, or a Speed indicated by the method of flight (in your ability or Wargear text, for example)
  • Flying ignores terrain penalties and can move over (but not through) other characters
  • If you are knocked Prone while flying, you fall from the sky (see Falling on p.201)

Climbing

  • ½ Speed
  • To climb faster – DN 3 Athletics (S) Test
    • Climb a number of metres up to Speed
  • Using Movement + Simple Action
    • Reduces DN by 1
  • Using Movement + Combat Action
    • Reduces DN by 2

Difficult Terrain

  • ½ Speed
