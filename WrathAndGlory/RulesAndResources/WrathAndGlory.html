--- title: Wrath & Glory ---

Wrath Die

  • 1 die in every dice pool is a Wrath Die
  • A 6 on the Wrath Die is a Wrath Critical
    • Add 1 Glory to the pool
    • If an attack – a Critical Hit is scored
  • A 1 on the Wrath Die is a Wrath Complication
    • Narrative complication to a scene

Wrath Points

  • Start with 2 each session
  • Rewarded by the GM for RP

Reroll Failures

  • After rolling, spend to roll every 1, 2, and 3
  • Once per Test
  • Can’t reroll a Wrath Complication

Narrative Declaration

  • Add a minor narrative element to the current scene
  • GM veto

Restore Shock

  • As an action spend to recover Shock equal to Rank + Tier
  • Only if conscious

Glory

  • Collective pool
  • Start each session at 0
  • Once per test an Exalted Icon can be shifted to add 1 Glory to the pool
  • Add 1 to the pool for each Wrath Critical
  • Max pool of 6 or number of players + 2, whichever is highest
  • Glory can be spent

Increase Dice Pool

  • Add 1 bonus dice to a Test per Glory spent
  • Only after the initial roll and any reroll of failures using Wrath
  • Bonus dice cannot be rerolled
  • Once per test

Increase Damage

  • +1 damage per Glory spent on successful attack

Increase Severity of a Critical Hit

  • Spend Glory to increase effect of a critical hit

Seize Initiative

  • Spend Glory to seize the initiative during combat
