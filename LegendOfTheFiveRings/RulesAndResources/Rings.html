Ring Approaches Narrative Opportunities Examples
Air Grace, cunning, precision, subtlety People’s strengths and weaknesses, emitions, small details in objects
Earth Resilience, patience, memory, calm The character’s own memories, history of people and objects, defensive positions
Fire Passion, invention, candor, ferocity People’s motivations, flashes of insight, conspicuous absences
Water Flexibility, awareness, efficiency, charm Environmental dangers, presence of useful terrain or objects, escape routes
Void Mysticism, intuition, instinct, wisdom Supernatural presences, flashes of premonition, instinctive warnings
