--- title: Sample TNs ---
TN Description
1 An easy task for most people, such as carrying half one’s weight or finding a misplaced item.
2 An average task, such as jumping a ten-foot ditch or recognising someone in disguise.
3 A difficult task, such as scaling a cliff without rope or finding a well-hidden object.
4 A very hard task, such as diving safely from the top off a waterfall or recalling someone’s exact words years later.
5 An extremely hard task, such as stirring a demoralised army to action or hurling someone using only one hand.
6 An extraordinary task, such as discerning someone’s whereabouts from the mud on their sandals or felling a tree with a single ax blow.
7 A heroic task, such as outwrestling a troll or naming all of one’s ancestors in order.
8+ A legendary task, such as shattering stone with one’s bare hands or outwitting a Fortune.
