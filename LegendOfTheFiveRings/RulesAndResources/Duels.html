Initiative Checks

TN 1 Meditation check

Common Forms of Duels

  • Iaijutsu Duel (to first strike or first blood)
  • Iaijutsu Duel (to the death)
  • Sparring Bout (to incapacitation)
  • Warrior’s Duel (to defeat)
  • Taryu-Jiai (to incapacitation)

Staredown

  • Special step at the start of Phase 2: Take Turns
  • Receive strife equal to the number of the round
  • Bid additional strife (between 0 and their focus attribute) and adds to initiative

Finishing Blow

  • First time their opponent becomes Compromised or unmasks during a duel
  • Interrupts and can be interrupted by another finishing blow
  • Attack action
  • If successful, no damage, but critical strike with severity equal to two times the deadliness of the weapon or Attack action used, plus the attacker’s bonus successes
  • Any other effects of the Attack action are resolved as usual
  • Lower honour for tie break
