--- title: Advantages and Disadvantages ---

Common Categories Effects

Distinction

The player chooses up to 2 dice to reroll.

Adversity

The player must choose 2 dice with results containing (success) and (explosive success) symbols to reroll. If they fail, they gain 1 Void point.

Passion

After resolving the check, the character removes 3 strife.

Anxiety

After the check, the character receives 3 strife. The first time this occurs each scene, they gain 1 Void point.
